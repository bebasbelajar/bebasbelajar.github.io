3. crocodiles ...in a swamp. a.live. b.lives. c.lived. d living​


Jawab: A. Live

Pembahasan:

Crocodiles ...in a swamp.

a.live = utk subject Plural (jamak)

b.lives = utk subject singular (tunggal)

c.lived = Past Tense

d.living​ = Continues Tense

* Alasannya memilih Jawaban A. Live

✅ Subjek jamak crocodile(s)

✅ kalimat bentuk Present Tense karena berisi sebuah Fakta (kebenaran umum)

* Berdasarkan Rumus:

(+) S + VERB (-s/es) + O/C

= Crocodiles + live + in swamp

= Crocodile + lives + in swamp

Structure Kalimat:

• Subject

Crocodiles = Buaya-buaya (jamak)

Crocodile = Buaya (tunggal)

• VERB 1

Live = hidup/tinggal (utk subject jamak)

Live+s = hidup/tinggal (subject tunggal)

• O/C

In swamp = di rawa

JADI:

Karena subject Plural (jamak) crocodile(s) maka Jawaban adalah A. Live

Jawaban:

1. Why are crocodiles predators?

= Because crocodiles need animal meat to survive and as food

2. In what areas do crocodiles live?

= Crocodiles generally inhabit freshwater habitats such as rivers, lakes, swamps and other wetlands

3. Why do crocodiles have good survival?

= Because Crocodiles save energy by eating meat that is large enough and contains a lot of protein to last for some time to come. This is also why this species can survive in the past when food on earth was so scarce.

4. What is surprising about crocodiles, dealing with their appearonces?

= Besides having a fat body, but with a long and thin muzzle. Crocodiles have highly coordinated prey hunting tactics.

5. What do crocodiles eat?

= Crocodiles are carnivores or meat eaters and eat fish, birds, frogs, and others

27. 20. A crocodile commonly ... in a swamp. a. lives b. living c. lived d. live ​


Jawaban:

Hi, kakak bantu jawab pertanyaanmu yah :)

Jawabannya adalah a. live.

Perintah soal yaitu menentukan kata kerja yang tepat.

Kalimat tersebut menggunakan Simple Present Tense. Rumusnya adalah S + V1 (s/es). Penambahan "s/es" pada V1 diberikan jika subjek kalimat tersebut subjek She/He/It dan subjek tunggal (singular).

"Crocodiles" merupakan subjek jamak. Dengan demikian kalimat soal menjadi "Crocodiles live in a swamp" (Buaya hidup di rawa-rawa.)

Jadi, jawaban yang benar = a. live.
