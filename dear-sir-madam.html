Jawaban:

Yes, that is correct. When writing an application letter, the way you address the recipient in the salutation can affect the closing. 21. What's the difference from Mr, Sir, Ms, Mrs, Miss, Madam, Ma'am??​


1. Mr: It is a shortened form of "Mister" and is used when addressing a man that you don't know very well.

2. Sir: It is generally used for a man of any age that is older or higher in authority.

3. Ms: It is used for a woman regardless of her status.

4. Mrs: It is used for a woman that is married.

5. Miss: It is used for a woman that is not yet married.

6. Madam: It is generally used for a woman that is older or higher in authority.

7. Ma'am: It is a shortened form of Madam.
