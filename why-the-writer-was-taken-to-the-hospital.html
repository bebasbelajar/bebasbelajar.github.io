26. As the victim was badly hurt in the car accident, he ..... to the nearest hospital A. will be taken B. is being taken C. has been taken D. was taken E. took


Kelas : XI
Pelajaran : bahasa Inggris
Kategori : passive voice
Kata kunci : past passive voice, tense

Pembahasan:
Perhatikan to be pada soal:
As the victim WAS badly hurt in the car accident, he ..... to the nearest hospital.

To be pada soal di atas berbentuk PAST TENSE, yang artinya ketika korban terluka parah dalam kecelakaan mobil, dia (dibawa) ke rumah sakit terdekat.

Dia (dibawa) ke rumah sakit terdekat, berbentuk kalimat pasif. Dengan bentuk lengkap : dia (dibawa) ke rumah sakit oleh seseorang.

Karena kalimat pertama berbentuk past (lampau), maka kalimat kedua berbentuk passive past sentence (object + was/were + v3 + by subject).

Jadi, jawabannya :
As the victim was badly hurt in the car accident, he WAS TAKEN to the nearest hospital (D).

Pilihan A. Will be taken, berbentuk passive future tense (object + will be + v3 + by subject)
Pilihan B. Is being taken, berbentuk passive present continous tense (object + am/is/are + being + v3 + by subject)
Pilihan C. Has been taken, berbentuk passive present perfect tense (object + has/have + been + v3 + by subject)
Pilihan E. Took, berbentuk active past tense (subject + v2), yang bila diartikan adalah membawa.
