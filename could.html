Jawaban:

Teacher : Good morning students.

Students : Good morning mam.

Teacher : Today we will study about simple past tense. We use simple past tense for something that happened in the past. Do you understand students?

Students : Yes, mam.

Teacher : Hana, could you repeat again what i say?

Hana : Sorry mam. I didn't hear what you say just now. Could you explain it again please?

Teacher : We use simple past tense for something that happened in the past. Until here understand Hana?

Hana : Yes, mam. Thank you for your explanation.

Penjelasan:

Could merupakan salah satu contoh dari modal

Contoh-contoh modal yaitu :

•Can & Could : Bisa.

•May : Boleh

•Will & Would : Akan

•Must : Harus

• etc

Rumus Modal :

1. Verb

(+) S + Modal + Verb

(-) S + Modal + Not + Verb

(?) Modal + S + Verb + ?

2. NonVerb

(+) S + Modal + be + nonverb

(-) S + Modal + not + be + nonverb

(?) Modal + S + be + nonverb + ?

26. apa perbedaan could dan would ?


Jawaban:

Would: bentuk lampau dari will (akan), digunakan untuk situasi tidak nyata dan meminta secara sopan.

Sedangkan,

Could: bentuk lampau dari can (bisa), digunakan untuk menyatakan kemungkinan dan meminta secara sopan.
