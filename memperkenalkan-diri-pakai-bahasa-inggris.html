10. tolong translate ke bahasa inggris.(jangan pakai app/web penerjemah)"selamat siang, kami dari kelompok 2 ingin mempresentasikan hasil diskusi kami""tapi sebelum memulai, ijinkan kami untuk memperkenalkan diri kami masing-masing""mari kita mulai"


"Good afternoon, we from group 2 want to present the results of our discussion"

"but before starting, allow us to introduce ourselves."

"Let's start

Menerjemahkan kalimat pembuka untuk presentasi adalah sebagai berikut:

"Good afternoon, we are from group two will present our discussion result."

"But before we begin, please let us introduce ourselves..."

"Let's begin."


Pembahasan

Sebelum kita melakukan presentasi, hal pertama yang harus dilakukan adalah mengucapkan kalimat pembukaan. Biasanya kalimat pembukaan berisi perkenalan diri setiap anggota beserta topik yang akan dibahas pada presentasi.


Berikut ini adalah contoh kalimat pembuka lainnya yang bisa kalian pelajari:

1. Good morning, my name is XXX I'm the leader of group 1. In this occasion we are going to present the result of ..... that we have discussed earlier. But before we get into it, firstly, we would like to introduce ourselves....

Alright, now that we are all set, let's start this presentation. I hope you will enjoy.


2. Good afternoon, today our group is going to present about ........ but firstly me as the leader of group will introduce the members. Our group consist of ...... as the ......, ....... as the ........, and ....... as the ........ . If you have any question please feel free to ask only during the question and answer segment. Many thanks.
