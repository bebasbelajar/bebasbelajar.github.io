15. Please make a dialogue by using Polite Requests (would you please, would you like and would you mind)


Jawaban:

Adam: "Hi Boby, How is it going?"

Boby: "I'm good, Adam. What's the matter?"

Adam: "I want to borrow your book. Would you mind if I borrow your book? "

Boby: "Absolutely Adam, come in. "

Adam: "Thank you, Boby. "

Boby: "By the way, would you like some tea? "

Adam: "I would like to drink some tea. "

Boby: "Here is your glass of tea, and this is my book that you want to borrow."

Adam: "Would you please turn on the fan? It is kind of hot here"

Boby: "Sure, there you go"

Adam: "Thanks a lot, Boby"

Penjelasan:
