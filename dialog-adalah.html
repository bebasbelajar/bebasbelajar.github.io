18. Jika alog x = p, alog y = q, dan alog z = r, Nilai dari alog x2y4z3 adalah ....please answer !!....


a log (x^2 × y^4 × z^3)
= a log x^2 + a log y^4 + a log z^3
= 2 (a log x) + 4 (a log y) + 3 (a log z)
= 2p + 4q + 3r
