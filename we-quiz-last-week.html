6. We studied english last week bentuk pasif ny


Jawaban:

English was studied by us last week

Pembahasan

We studied english last week

Structure Kalimatnya:Subject => weVerb-2 => studied Object => English Adverb of time => last week

Berdasarkan keterangan di atas:Kalimat bentuk aktif karena Subjek melakukan aksi dalam kalimatnya.Kalimat menggunakan Past Tense karena terdapat waktu Lampau (last week)

Jadi, Rumus yang digunakan

Active-Past Tense

(+) S + V2 + O

=> We studied english last week

Passive-Past Tense

(+) O + Be (was/were) + V3 + by S

=> English was studied by us last week

Pendahuluan

Kalimat pada soal meggunakan keterangan waktu "last week" itu artinya kalimat tersebut adalah bentuk Past Tense (Waktu lampau).

Rumus Simple Past Tense

        [tex]\boxed{S + Verb2 + O}[/tex]

Dalam  simple past tense terdapat dua jenis kata kerja :

Reguler Verb, tambahkan -ed/-d

        Contoh :

         a. Move ⇒ Moved

         b. Play ⇒ Played

         c. Punch ⇒ Punched

         d. Match ⇒ Matched

         e. Mix ⇒ Mixed

Irreguler Verb, kata kerja kedua berbeda dengan yang pertama

        Contoh :

        a. Eat ⇒ ate

        b. Run ⇒ Ran

        c. Begin ⇒ Began

        d. Drink ⇒ Drank

        e. Awake ⇒ Awoke

Kembali ke soal.............

Soal :

we(move)....to a new house last week​

Jawaban :

30. we ... (learn) english at this time last week​


at this time : continuous tense

last week : past tense

berarti soal ini menggunakan past continuous tense

Subject + was/were + V1 + ing

I, he, she, it + was

you, we, they + were

we (were learning) English at this time last week

From the term of LAST, we can know that it is PAST TENSE which, use VERB 2 which many of the irregular verbs change the words completely so we Add BE which is in the past form and plural form which is WERE because the subject is WE. And add LEARNING because there is the word: At this time.
