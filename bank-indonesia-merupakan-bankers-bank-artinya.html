BANKING

In the Babylonian, Greek and Roman times successful banking ventures had held the role of trade traffic. The task of the bank at that time was more precisely exchanging money, so people called money traders. In general, money traders can only exchange foreign currency with their own country's currency or vice versa. Then this business develops by accepting savings, depositing, then buying money by collecting loan interest.

The beginning of the establishment of banks in the World can be briefly described as follows. about 2000 BC in Babylon was known as a kind of bank. This bank lends gold and silver at an interest rate of 20% every month and is known as the Temples of Babylon. After the Babylon era, 500 BC followed in Greece by establishing a bank, known as the Greek Temple, which accepted deposits by collecting storage fees and lending them back to the community. That was when the first private bankers emerged. Its operations include exchanging money and all kinds of bank activities. The first banking institution in Greece arose in 560 BC.

After the Greek era, the emergence of a bank business in Rome whose operations were even broader, namely the exchange of currencies, receiving deposits, giving credit, transferring capital and along with the fall of the Roman city in 509 BC, banking also fell. but in 527-565 AD Justin codified Roman law in Constatinople so that banking flourished. this development began with trade with China, India and Ethiopia. Even the Constatinople currency is determined as an international currency. Trade relations then developed into West Asia (now the Middle East) and Europe so that cities such as Alexandria, Venice and several ports in Southern Italy were known as important trade centers. 22. Jelaskan pengertian dari bankers acceptance


Banker’s Acceptance (BA) atau aksep adalah instrumen yang diciptakan untuk memfasilitasi transaksi perdagangan komersial. BA berkaitan dengan transaksi tertentu dengan barang yang mendasarinya. Nilai barang yang mendasarinya tercermin dalam nilai nominal tagihan yang mewakili jumlah yang dijanjikan oleh mitra bisnis untuk dibayar pada waktu tertentu di masa depan.

Ketika bank menerima tanggung jawab untuk membayar kreditur jika debitur gagal untuk membayar kembali maka disebut mendiskontokan BA. BA dipandang sebagai investasi yang sangat aman karena tidak hanya dijamin bank tetapi juga mewakili transaksi bisnis alami dengan barang yang mendasarinya.
