14. The participants have had some problems deciding.....A. when they should announce the result of the meeting.B. when are they going to announce the result of the meeting.C. When should they announce the result of the meeting..D. the time when the result of the meeting to announce.​


Jawaban:

A

Penjelasan:

subject dulu terus auxilliary/modal/to be

NOUN CLAUSE

The participants have had some problems deciding when they should announce the result of the meeting. (A)

------------

EXPLANATION

Noun clause adalah sebuah dependent clause (anak kalimat) yang berfungsi sebagai kata benda.

Noun clause dapat digantikan dengan kata ganti 'it'.

Noun clause diawali dengan how, that, what, whatever, when, where, if, whether, which, whichever, who, whoever, whom, whomever, atau why.

Meskipun diawali dengan kata tanya, noun clause bukanlah kalimat tanya (interrogative) melainkan pernyataan (declarative). Oleh karena itu, konstruksi noun clause adalah:

QW + S + v bukan QW + auxiliary verb + S + verb

Contoh:

I know when he will come.  ✓

I know when will he come. X

-----------

EXPLICATION

Setelah memahami apa itu noun clause, mari kita bahas satu persatu opsi jawaban:

A. when they should announce the result of the meeting.

declarative (S + verb)  → noun clause

B. when are they going to announce the result of the meeting.

interrogative (auxiliary + S)  → bukan noun clause

C. When should they announce the result of the meeting.

interrogative (auxiliary + S)  → bukan noun clause

D. the time when the result of the meeting to announce.

Bukan klausa karena tidak mempunyai finite verb.

17. They have ___ the meeting since about two hours agoA.Begin C.BegunB.Began D.Beginning​


Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Materi: Present Perfect Tense

Jawaban:

C. Begun

Penjelasan:

Rumus Present Perfect Tense:

S + have/has + v3 + O

V3 atau past participle dari kata kerja "mulai" adalah begun. Sehingga kalimat sempurnanya adalah:

They have begun the meeting since about two hours ago
