CONDITIONAL SENTENCES

Part A.

1. My mother is not a good cook, so she doesn't make me a cake.

➡ If my mother were a good cook, she would make me a cake.

2. I don't sleep till afternoon, because today is not Sunday.

➡ If today were Sunday, I would sleep till afternoon.

3. Today is not a free time, so I do not go to the grandmother's house.

➡ If today were a free time, I would go to the grandmother's house.

Part B.

1. I am busy next week. If I had enough time, I would come to your party

➡ I don't come to your party because I'm busy.

EXPLANATION

Conditional sentence adalah kalimat andai², di mana suatu keinginan dapat terwujud apabila persyaratannya terpenuhi. Conditional sentence tersusun atas if clause dan main clause/result clause. If clause menggambarkan kondisinya, dan main clause menggambarkan konsekuensinya.

Conditional type 1 [First Conditional]

↪menjabarkan keinginan yang kemungkinan terjadi saat ini ataupun nanti-nanti (real possibility) berdasarkan prediksi.

Formula :

If+Simple present, Simple future

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will+verb1......

Exp.

If she cleans her room, she will find the key.

Fact ➡ She probably finds the key.

Conditional type 2 [Second conditional] ↪menjabarkan keinginan yang diragukan terjadi saat ini atau mendatang (hypothetical situation). Pakai auxiliary BE "were" utk semua bentuk subyek sbg bentuk harapan yg blm tentu terwujud (Subjunctive).

Formula :

If+Simple past, Past future

If+S+verb2 ......, S+would+verb1......

Exp.

If I were you, I would tell the truth to your mom.

Fact ➡ I don't tell the truth because I'm not you.

Conditional tipe 3 [Third conditional] ↪menjabarkan keinginan yang tidak terwujud di waktu lampau (truly hypothetical). Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah sebagai dampak dari kejadian di waktu lampau.

Formula :

If+Past perfect, Past future perfect

If+S+had+verb3...., S+would+have+verb3....

Exp.

If he had kept the promise, his girl friend wouldn't have been disappointed.

Fact ➡ he didn't keep the promise, so girl friend was disappointed.

Pembahasan

Kalimat-kalimat tersebut termasuk dalam Conditional Sentence atau Kalimat Pengandaian/Bersyarat. Dalam kalimat ini terdapat dua elemen yang perlu diperhatikan, yaitu if-clause dan main clause.

Secara umum, kalimat ini terbagi atas 3 jenis, yaitu:

Type 1

Kalimat ini biasa digunakan untuk membicarakan situasi nyata yang besar kemungkinannya terjadi di masa depan.

Formula:

if-clause: Simple Present main clause: will-future or (Modal + infinitive)

Contoh:

If it rains, I will not go to the park.If we don't eat, we will be hungry.

Type 2

Kalimat ini biasa digunakan untuk membicarakan situasi tidak realistis yang mustahil atau mungkin tidak terjadi di masa depan.

Formula:

if-clause: Simple Past main clause: would + infinitive *

Contoh:

If I were a billionaire, I would travel the entire space.If you had money , you would build a zoo for this city.

Type 3

Kalimat ini biasa digunakan untuk menggambarkan situasi pada masa lalu yang tidak terjadi dan kebalikan dari kondisi pada saat ini.

Formula:

if-clause: Past Perfect main clause: would + have + past participle *

Contoh:

If you had told me to go, I would have not waited for you here.If I had known about the scholarship, I would have applied for it.
