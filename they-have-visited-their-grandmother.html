30. Ana and adi visited their grandmother last month Into negative? Interrogative?


Jawaban :

Ana and Adi visited their grandmother last month.

(-) Ana and Adi did not visit their gradmother last month.

(?) Did Ana and Adi visit their grandmother last month?

PEMBAHASANSIMPLE PAST

↪ Tenses yang menjelaskan tentang kegiatan yang terjadi pada masa sekarang. Ciri-ciri dari tenses ini adalah pada kalimat positifnya menggunakan verb-2, sedangkan untuk kalimat negatif

dan interrogatif menggunakan auxiliary verb did.

Rumus :

(+) S + V2 + O

We watered the plants yesterday. She drank a cup of tea.

(-) S + Did + not + V1 + O

We did not water the plants yesterdayShe did not drink a cup of tea

(?) Did + S + V1 + O

Did we water the plants yesterday? Did she drink a cup of tea?

Tanda Waktu :YesterdayLast monthThree years agoTwo hours agoDan sebagainya

KESIMPULAN

Pada kalimat positif di soal tersebut kita ketahui bahwa setelah subject "Ana and Adi", kata kerjanya menggunaan verb-2 dari "visit" yaitu visited. Maka rumus negatif dan interrogatifnya menggunakan rumus simple past seperti yang telah disebutkan diatas.

(+) Ana and Adi visited their grandmother last month.

➡ Ana dan Adi mengunjungi nenek mereka bulan lalu.

Ana and Adi => SubjectVisited => Verb-2Their grandmother => ObjectLast month => Adverb of time

(-) Ana and Adi did not visit their grandmother last month.

➡ Ana dan Adi tidak mengunjungi nenek mereka bulan lalu

Ana and Adi => SubjectDid => Auxiliary verb (past) Not => Tanda negatifVisit => Verb-1Their grandmother => ObjectLast month => Adverb of time

(?) Did Ana and Adi visit their grandmother last month?

➡ Apakah Ana dan Adi mengunjungi nenek mereka bulan lalu?

Did => Auxiliary verbAna and Adi => SubjectVisit => Verb-1Their grandmother => ObjectLast month => Adverb of time
